Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026

At SignalFlow, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

When you sign up for SignalFlow beta access, we collect:

  • First Name — to personalize your experience
  • Email Address — to send you trading signals and service updates
  • Instrument Preferences — the trading instruments you selected (XAUUSD, SP500, NAS100)
  • Timeframe Preferences — your selected signal timeframes (15m, 1H)
  • Sign-up Date — when you joined the beta

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Send you trading signals for your selected instruments and timeframes
  • Send service-related announcements and updates
  • Personalize your email communications
  • Improve our signal delivery service

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely in a PostgreSQL database hosted on Railway (railway.app). All data is encrypted in transit using SSL/TLS.

4. Email Communications

We use SendGrid to deliver emails. Your email address is shared with SendGrid solely for the purpose of delivering our service emails. SendGrid's privacy policy can be found at sendgrid.com/policies/privacy.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of your personal data
  • Update — correct any inaccurate information
  • Delete — request deletion of your account and data
  • Unsubscribe — opt out of email communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at YourWinningNow@gmail.com.

6. Cookies

SignalFlow does not currently use cookies or tracking technologies on our landing page.

7. Children's Privacy

SignalFlow is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify registered users of significant changes via email.
